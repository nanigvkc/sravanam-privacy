Privacy Policy for Sravanam

Last Updated: March 2026

Welcome to Sravanam. We are committed to providing a safe, educational, and secure environment for children and their families. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in our mobile application.

Children’s Privacy Commitment: Sravanam complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and the 2026 App Store Accountability Acts (ASAA). We do not collect personal information directly from children.

1. Information We Collect

When you use Sravanam, we may collect certain information to provide and improve the service:

  • Account Information: If you use Google Sign-In, we receive your name, email address, and a unique User ID. This is used solely to manage your account and sync progress across devices.
  • App Usage Data: We collect non-identifiable information about how the app is used (e.g., which lessons are completed) to improve our educational content.
  • Device Information: Basic device details (model, OS version) for troubleshooting and security.

2. How We Use Data

We use the collected data to:

  • Personalize the learning experience for children via parent-managed profiles.
  • Ensure the app functions correctly on your specific device.
  • Verify age categories through the Play Age Signals API to maintain a child-safe environment.

3. Data Sharing and Security

We do not sell your data. We only share information with third parties (like Google Play Services) necessary to run the app. All data is encrypted in transit using industry-standard protocols (HTTPS/TLS).

  • Timeline: Data will be permanently removed from our active servers within 30 days.
